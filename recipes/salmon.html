Italian-Herb Salmon Recipe

Delicious Recipe You Never Knew You Needed

I am a bit obsessed with Salmon. This is just a recipe I conjured up because I was so tired of eating out to get my Salmon fix. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 filets (fresh or frozen) Salmon
  • Avocado Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Red Chilli Flakes
  • Italian-Herb Seasoning
  • 2 Tbs of Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Add about 1 Tbs of Avocado oil (or whatever cooking oil you prefer) to your pan on medium-low heat
  2. Season your Salmon on each side with Kosher Salt. I didn't add any measurements to any of the seasonings because I usually just add enough to cover just enough of the entire side. Not too much but not too little
  3. After adding Kosher Salt to both sides of each Filet, you will then do the same for the Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Red Chilli Flakes, and Italian-Herb Seasoning
  4. Your oil should be hot by now so we can just go ahead and add the Filets to the pan
  5. You will want to cook your salmon for about 6-7 minutes on each side
  6. Once it's cooked thoroughly you can let it cool for about 5 minutes and serve
