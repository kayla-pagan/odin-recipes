Lasagna Recipe

Easy Homemade Lasagna

This recipe has been passed on from generation to generation in my family. If you would like to know a quick and easy way to make the best lasagna ever, please read on.

Ingredients

  • 2 Boxes of Lasagna Noodles
  • 64 oz of Chicken Broth
  • 24 oz of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Pounds of Ground Beef
  • 32 oz of Ricotta Cheese
  • 8 Cups of Shredded Cheese
  • 2 Tb Salted Butter
  • 1 Cup of Diced Onions
  • 3 Tb of Minced Garlic
  • 2 Tb of Sofrito
  • 1 Tb of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tb of Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp of Cumin
  • 1 Tsp of Tumeric
  • 1 Tsp of Black Pepper
  • 2 Tb of Adobo
  • 1 Tb of Sazon
  • 1 cup of Pecorino-Romano Cheese

Steps

  1. Add your 2 Tbs of Butter and your 1 cup onions and allow them to brown over low heat
  2. Add in your 3 Tbs of Minced Garlic along with the 2 Tbs of Sofrito and allow it to simmer for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add in your 2 pounds of ground beef and begin to break apart until it is all separated
  4. Once it's separated, begin to add your Adobo, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Cumin, Tumeric, and Black Pepper
  5. Let it cook thoroughly on medium-low heat until it's all browned
  6. Once it's evenly cooked, add your Tomato Sauce, Sazon, and Pecorino-Romano Cheese
  7. The meat is pretty much done at this point. Set it aside and put your Chicken Broth to boil
  8. Add in your lasagna noodles once it's boiling and lower the heat to medium-high
  9. Once your noodles are done drain the broth and immediately rinse them with cool water to stop them from cooking any further
  10. Now we are ready to assemble the Lasanga. Take some excess sauce from the cooked meat and add it to the bottom of your dish. This will prevent the noodles on the bottom from sticking
  11. Then, you will add your first layer of Noodles making sure the entire surface is covered
  12. Spead a generous amount of Ricotta Cheese onto the entire layer of noodles you laid down
  13. Add a layer of your ground beef
  14. Sprinkle your shredded cheese on top of your ground beef layer
  15. Repeat steps 12-15 until you get to the top of your dish
  16. The last layer on the top will be Lasanga Noodles. You will sprinkle this with a generous helping of shredded cheese
  17. Set your oven to 375 degrees and you will cook this for 40-50 minutes
  18. Let it cool for about 15 minutes and enjoy!
